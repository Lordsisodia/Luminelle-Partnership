## Primary Persona: "Style Preserver"

### Profile
- **Name**: Maya
- **Age**: 32
- **Location**: Atlanta, GA
- **Occupation**: Marketing Manager
- **Income**: $75,000/year

### Hair Context
- **Hair Type**: 4A (tight coils)
- **Current Routine**: Silk press every 2 weeks ($80-100)
- **Washing**: Twice weekly (would prefer less)
- **Styling Time**: 1.5 hours for wash day
- **Pain Point**: Silk press only lasts 5-7 days before reverting

### Behaviors
- **Research**: Reads reviews, watches YouTube, asks in Facebook groups
- **Social**: Active on Instagram, follows hair influencers
- **Purchase**: Quality-focused, willing to pay for what works
- **Loyalty**: Returns to products that deliver
- **Advocacy**: Tells friends about good finds

### Goals
1. Extend silk press to 14+ days (double current longevity)
2. Reduce hair damage from heat and washing
3. Simplify hair care routine
4. Feel confident daily without restyling

### Frustrations
- "Shower steam ruins everything"
- "Tired of spending so much on salon visits"
- "Regular shower caps don't protect from steam"
- "Don't want to wrap my hair every shower"

### What She Values
- Quality and effectiveness
- Aesthetic appeal (not ugly)
- Ease of use
- Community validation (reviews)
- Transparency (will it work for MY hair?)

### Decision Triggers
- Real customer photos with her hair type
- Clear explanation of HOW it works
- Social proof (reviews, ratings)
- Money-back guarantee
- Influencer recommendations

### Objections to Overcome
- "Will this work for 4A hair?"
- "Is it comfortable to wear?"
- "How long will it really last?"
- "Is it worth $40?"
- "Will I look silly wearing it?"

### Her Journey on Lumelle Site
1. **Lands on homepage** via Instagram ad
2. **Scans quickly** for photos of similar hair types
3. **Reads reviews** filtering for 4A hair
4. **Watches video** of how it works
5. **Checks FAQ** about sizing, care, returns
6. **Adds to cart** after feeling confident
7. **Purchases** if she feels certain it will work

### What Would Delight Her
- Photos of customers with 4A hair
- "Works for all hair types" demonstrated
- Clear before/after results
- Easy returns if it doesn't work
- Community of similar users

## Persona Summary for AI

When generating improvements, consider which persona(s) the feature is for:

### Style Preserver (Maya) - 70% of users
- **Needs**: Social proof, hair type validation, detailed info
- **Responds to**: Reviews, photos, education, community
- **Priorities**: Effectiveness, quality, will it work for ME?
- **Key features**: Reviews, photos, hair type guidance, education

### Convenience Seeker (Sarah) - 20% of users
- **Needs**: Speed, simplicity, clarity
- **Responds to**: Quick value prop, convenience features
- **Priorities**: Time savings, ease of use, low effort
- **Key features**: Quick buy, subscription, clear benefits, fast checkout

### Gift Buyer (James) - 10% of users
- **Needs**: Gift guidance, bundles, reassurance
- **Responds to**: Gift messaging, bundles, easy returns
- **Priorities**: Will they like it? Is it appropriate?
- **Key features**: Gift bundles, gift wrapping, gift receipt, returns
