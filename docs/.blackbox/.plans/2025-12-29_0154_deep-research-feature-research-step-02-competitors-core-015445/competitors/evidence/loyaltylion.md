## 2 workflows worth copying (step-by-step)

### Workflow A — Data-driven loyalty program setup (merchant admin)

1) Install LoyaltyLion and connect Shopify store data.
2) Define loyalty earning and reward mechanics (points/rewards are baseline).
3) Configure VIP/subscriber tiers for exclusivity and retention.
4) Wire loyalty events into marketing flows (e.g., Klaviyo) for automated lifecycle nudges.
5) Iterate based on “data-driven” program performance.
