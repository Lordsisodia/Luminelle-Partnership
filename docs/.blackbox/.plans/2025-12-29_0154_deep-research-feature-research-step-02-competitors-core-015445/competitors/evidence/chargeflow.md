## 2 workflows worth copying (step-by-step)

### Workflow A — Chargeback automation onboarding (merchant admin)

1) Install Chargeflow via Shopify App Store and connect payments providers.
2) Turn on chargeback alerts to catch disputes early (“prevent up to 90%…” positioning).
3) Enable evidence automation so disputes generate “science-based” evidence packages.
4) Monitor chargeback analytics dashboard and iterate prevention settings.

Evidence:
- `.blackbox/.plans/2025-12-29_0154_deep-research-feature-research-step-02-competitors-core-015445/competitors/snapshots-variants/chargeflow-shopify-app-store.html`

### Workflow B — Post-dispute learning loop (fraud + ops)

1) Review chargeback reasons and outcomes in unified analytics.
2) Identify patterns (friendly fraud / abusive customers).
3) Adjust prevention controls and alert rules.
4) Repeat weekly/monthly.

## 3 “steal ideas” (easy/medium/hard)

- Easy: merchant-readable “alerts” that quantify impact (e.g., the “prevent up to 90%” story anchored to outcomes).
- Medium: automated evidence generation that is explainable and customizable per dispute type.
- Hard: cross-provider dispute automation that safely spans multiple payment processors with consistent audit trails.
