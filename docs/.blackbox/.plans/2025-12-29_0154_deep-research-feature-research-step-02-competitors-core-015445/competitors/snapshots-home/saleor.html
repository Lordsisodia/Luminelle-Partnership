    But let's be honest — not for everyone

    Saleor is for commerce teams that want control and flexibility. More freedom, more power… and yes, a bit more responsibility and commitment. We might not be for you if:

    Looking for out-of-the box

    If you're looking for something highly opinionated that comes ready as a black box of commerce, keep sailing. Saleor is for those who need full control, ownership, and a tech stack that answers to you—not the other way around.

    Hate working with engineers

    Then we're not your crew. Saleor is built for builders. We collaborate deep—because great commerce requires great architecture.

    Love tight coupling

    Your engineers may not want to work in the programming language they're already proficient with, right? They may not either care about the industry standards. And maybe they love all those obscure DSLs to be part of their job.

    Love proprietary standards

    Who doesn’t!? Well, we don’t. Saleor is for those who prefer loose coupling, flexible service oriented architectures, high degree of independence for individual teams and developers.

    Prefer to go alone

    You can. We're open source. But if you want a battle-tested crew that's scaled through Black Fridays and 8-figure peaks, we're on deck. All hands, no hype.
