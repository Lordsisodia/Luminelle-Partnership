Frequently asked questions

How does Signifyd work? Expand

Signifyd collects session data like IP, behavior & transactional data, then uses machine learning to apply a contextual layer that includes forensic data and 150+ out of band ID sources. It then correlates all of this data to create a complete picture and to provide an accurate fraud decision. All of which allows Signifyd to fully shift the liability from the merchant to Signifyd with its 100% Guaranteed Fraud Protection.

How much is Signifyd? Expand

Signifyd charges a percentage of the order total when an order is approved. What that percentage is varies depending on the specific products purchased, merchant vertical, order volume, and average ticket price, among other metrics. There is no charge when an order is declined due to fraud. Learn more about Signifyd products.

How will you determine the exact cost for my business? Expand

Exact pricing is determined through an assessment of your current operating efforts, chargeback losses and order declines. Together, we’ll hone in on desired approval rate targets that make sense for your business and industry. Our pricing model consistently ensures a positive economic impact for merchants, regardless of the specific transaction cost or percentage of GMV.

Is Signifyd pricing flexible? Expand

Our goal is to find a solution that works for your business and sets you up for long-term success. To that end, we’ll never try to win your business with a low introductory rate that skyrockets down the road, and we will collaborate with you to customize a package that fits your team’s priorities – for today and tomorrow.

What does Signifyd contractually guarantee? Expand

Signifyd offers a configurable financial guarantee against chargebacks, ranging from fraud-only protection to a total chargeback liability shift. This differs from a performance SLA guarantee, which promises adherence to a specific approval rate without the built-in incentive to surpass them. Learn more about SLAs vs liability shifts.

Who does Signifyd integrate with? Expand

There are common ways you can easily integrate Signifyd into your existing tech stack for seamless protection: 

  1. Pre-built order integrations: Deploy Signifyd using one of our plug-and-play solutions with partners, including 
    • Miva, BigCommerce, Adobe, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Shopify, Netsuite, Accertify, Vtex and more
  2. Payment integrations: Connect Signifyd with your payment system to automatically receive chargeback data, including
    • Adyen, Stripe, PayPal, Square, WorldPay, and more 
  3. APIs & SDKs: Build a custom integration using our API & SDK suite, including: 
    • REST APIs, Webhooks, JavaScript tags, Mobile SDKs, SDK Libraries
