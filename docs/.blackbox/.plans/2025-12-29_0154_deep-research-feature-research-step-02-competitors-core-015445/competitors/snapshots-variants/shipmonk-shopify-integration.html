Seamless fulfillment for your Shopify store

A deeply optimized Shopify integration built for operational complexity, not just order sync. Connect your Shopify store to ShipMonk and automate your entire fulfillment process — no coding or developer required. Our direct integration syncs products, orders, and inventory in real-time, so you can focus on growth, not logistics.

Get Started with ShipMonk

Powering growth for innovative brands.

Feature updates

  • Faster, more reliable syncs: Improved speed and accuracy during high-volume peaks.

  • Global compatibility: Automated tax ID mapping for international orders (Brazil, Mexico, China, South Korea).

  • Enhanced tag mapping: Trigger ShipMonk actions directly from Shopify tags (e.g. hold, prioritize, reroute).

  • Expanded location mapping: Assign Shopify locations to specific fulfillment centers with one click.

  • Certified Shopify Fulfillment Network (SFN) Partner: Officially recognized by Shopify for fulfillment excellence.

  • SFN transparency: Performance and coverage visibility directly in your Shopify SFN dashboard.

Advanced capabilities

Automation and control

  • Automatically route or hold orders based on Shopify tags like VIP, fraud_risk, or urgent.

  • Easily link Shopify locations to ShipMonk fulfillment centers with no developer needed.

  • Handle complex products, bundles, and kits with smart catalog rules by region or stock level.

 

Scale and reliability

  • Set buffer inventory to prevent overselling during peak seasons.

  • Let ShipMonk automatically split and fulfill orders from the optimal warehouse.

  • Monitor syncs and resolve issues instantly with detailed error visibility.

  • Streamline operations with integrated partner and vendor fulfillment management.

Shopify Fulfillment Network Partner

As a certified SFN partner, you can find and connect with us directly inside the Shopify Fulfillment Network app:

  • View ShipMonk as a top recommended 3PL partner.
  • See transparent pricing, real-time performance stats, and our network coverage.
  • Install our app or contact our team with a single click.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the integration work?
It fully automates your fulfillment by syncing orders, inventory, and tracking information between Shopify and ShipMonk in real-time.


Is it difficult to set up?
No. Setup takes just a few clicks from our platform, with no coding required. Our implementation team is here to help guide you.


What does being a Shopify Fulfillment Network (SFN) partner mean for me?
It means Shopify trusts our technology and service. You get a certified, high-performance 3PL partner seamlessly integrated into your workflow for faster, more reliable shipping.


How does the integration prevent overselling?
Our two-way, real-time inventory sync ensures that your stock levels are always accurate across both your Shopify store and our warehouses.
