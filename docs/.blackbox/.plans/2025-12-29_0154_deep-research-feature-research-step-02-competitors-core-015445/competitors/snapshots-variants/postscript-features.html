Segments

Turn conversations into conversions

Two-way messaging is where the magic of SMS happens.

Flow Builder

Flow Builder allows your brand to scale personalized one-to-one conversations with your subscribers. Easily build, visualize, and manage SMS campaigns and automations using our intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Create different follow-up responses based on how your subscribers reply or what actions they take, and easily compare the performance of any message using our in-flow analytics.

Campaigns
Powered by AI

Use Flow Builder to build campaigns that offer a variety of experiences for your customers, from flash sale announcements and holiday promotions to educational, interactive, or conversational message flows. With Postscript AI, you can be sure that youre always sending the best possible Campaign messageresulting in higher clickthrough rates, conversions, and overall earnings.

Automations
Powered by AI

Automatically send a messageor a flow of multiple messagesbased on predefined trigger events. Flow Builder enables brands to create action-based automations such as welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase, order shipped, subscription management, back in stock, loyalty offer, and review request messages. With Postscript AI, you can combine predictive analytics and generative AI technology to generate and test thousands of on-brand message variants that evolve over time in order to drive an average 20% revenue lift all proven by holdout testing.

Responses

Manage and respond to incoming subscriber texts via the Responses tab. We use machine learning to help you filter and prioritize conversations based on the sentiment ( Positive, Negative, or Neutral) or the topic (Question, Shipping, Returns, etc.). You can also forward subscriber replies to your support email, integrate with Slack, or connect Postscript to your help desk of choiceincluding Gorgias, Zendesk, and many others.

Analytics

Postscripts Analytics empowers you to visualize and interpret data so you can identify actionable insights, refine your messaging strategy and ultimately drive revenue. Weve thoughtfully crafted our analytics experience in partnership with leading Shopify brands in order to develop an experience thats SMS-first and meets the unique needs of Shopify merchants. Pair our dashboards with our data-driven team of ecommerce experts, who bring deep knowledge of the industry, and industry-leading SMS strategy, and youve got the perfect recipe to make SMS your #1 revenue channel.

Compliance features and experts

All of Postscripts features and list growth tools are hard-wired to follow guidelines set by the TCPA, mobile carriers, and other regulatory bodies wherever possible. Additionally, we have:

  • A team of in-house compliance and legal experts.

  • A partnership with the TCPA Defense Force so we can track industry regulations, proactively notify our customers of any changes, and help answer any of your compliance-related questions.

  • Direct relationships with the carrier networks to stay in the loop on any upcoming compliance changes as well as negotiate for better rates on behalf of our customers.

Infrastructure and deliverability

We strive to balance compliance regulations with deliverability so your messages not only follow all regulatory best practices, but also reach your subscribers. Thats why we:

  • Pre-pay for maximum throughput bandwidth, meaning sent messages reach subscribers faster.

  • Built our platform for scale, with infrastructure redundancies in place to handle traffic spikes (such as during BFCM).

  • Formed a deep partnership with Twilio to 3x bandwidth for our customersand prevent message failures and delays.

  • Continually obsess over promoting list hygiene and nurturing positive carrier relations.
