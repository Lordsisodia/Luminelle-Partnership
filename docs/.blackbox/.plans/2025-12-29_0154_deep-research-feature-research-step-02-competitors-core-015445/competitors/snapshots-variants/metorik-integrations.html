Integrations FAQs

Can't find what you're looking for below? Read our Help Docs

Do I need developer skills to set these integrations up?

Not at all. You can connect Metorik to your Shopify or WooCommerce store in just a few clicks. Setting up the integrations is straightforward with most integrations just requiring you to log in to the service and approve the connection.

If you do get stuck, we’ve got step-by-step documentation for every integration and our support team is just an email away if you need a hand with anything.

Can I connect Metorik to other eCommerce platforms?

Metorik currently integrates directly with Shopify and WooCommerce.

However, if you’ve fallen in love with Metorik and can’t live without it, you can connect Metorik to almost any platform by using a “ghost” WooCommerce site as a middleman layer, i.e. Metorik ↔ Ghost Site ↔ Amazon.

This “ghost site” approach will most likely require some help from a developer, and not every Metorik feature is supported.

Check out our guide on this here: Does Metorik work with all Platforms?

Are there any limits to the amount of data I can import?

For your Shopify & WooCommerce store, all your historical order data will be synced over to Metorik during the initial import.

We allow 120× your monthly order limit in our historical sync. So for Level 5 (10,000 orders per month), we will import up to 1.2 million historical orders.

If you're over the limit for your plan, Metorik will prompt you to upgrade to a higher plan.

For our integrations, we’ll sync over all the relevant available data going as far back as your very first order.

Are there any costs for these integrations?

There’s no additional costs for these integrations. We want to ensure every Metorik customer has access to all the possible tools they need to be able to grow their store, so like our features, you get access to everything, regardless of the plan level you’re on.

How often does Metorik sync with my store?

Metorik syncs with your store in two ways:

  • Your store notifies Metorik (via webhooks) whenever there’s new or updated data, and we’ll sync this over right away so you’re always seeing the latest. Depending on your website, there may be a delay of a minute or two.
  • We also do a second check every 15 minutes to ensure everything’s up to date, just in case your store failed to notify us of an update or a new order.

Learn more about Data Syncing in Metorik.

How often does data sync with these integrations?

This varies slightly depending on the specific integration, but most integrations will sync data over to Metorik in real-time, or every 15 minutes.

Some integrations, such as Google Analytics, delay making data available via their API. Because of this, you may occasionally see a temporary mismatch between the platform and what’s showing in Metorik when looking at the very latest information.
