Zendesk for customer service

The complete customer service solution

Let Zendesk AI help you resolve requests from day one. Bring together customer conversations from all channels, giving agents the context they need. Access dashboards and data easily to enhance your service quality.

Use your work email to connect customer emails, apps, and invite team members.

Benefits

Quality service, made simple

Give your customers the responsive support they expectthrough email, voice, messaging, or social media. Scale your team's capabilities with AI assistance that's always available, while our agent workspace helps agents efficiently manage and respond to every conversation.

Give agents a single workspace with complete customer views, predefined responses that save time, and seamless internal team collaborationall without switching tools or losing context.

Grow your service operation efficiently. Questions automatically route to the right team member. Automate what makes sense, measure what matters, and keep costs manageable as you expand.

Gather important data points in one place and get a complete view of your business. Uncover operational improvements with prebuilt dashboards, or build your own in minutes. Spot coaching opportunities, identify agent knowledge gaps, and benchmark your service against peers.
