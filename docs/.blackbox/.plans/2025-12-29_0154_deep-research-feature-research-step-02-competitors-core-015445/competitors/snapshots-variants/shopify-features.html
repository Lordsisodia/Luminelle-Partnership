Omnichannel selling

Offer a smooth shopping experience for your customers, in store, online, and everywhere in between

Pickup in store Pro

Manage and fulfill in-store pickup orders directly from Shopify POS.

Buy in store and ship Pro

Make sales in store and ship directly to customers from anywhere you have inventory. Taxes and shipping rates are automatically calculated at checkout.

Buy online, exchange and return in store Pro

Accept exchanges for purchases made online or at another location and your inventory will be updated instantly.

Local delivery Pro

Manage and fulfill local delivery orders placed online directly from your Shopify POS.

Product QR codes

Attach QR codes to products so customers can use their smartphone to learn more and purchase online.

Send cart to buy online Pro

Send customers an email with items they were interested in but didn’t purchase in store.

Ship from store Pro

Surface shipping orders for fulfillment in POS so staff can pick, pack and ship orders, including purchasing and printing shipping labels.

Manage orders in POS

Advanced inventory management

Reduce errors and increase profit from a single place using smart recommendations from Stocky app by Shopify

Purchase orders Pro

Create purchase orders for vendors and suppliers.

Demand forecasting Pro

Get purchase order suggestions based on recent product performance or seasonality.

Transfers Pro

Request transfers from your other locations and keep track of what’s been received and what’s leaving.

Low stock reports Pro

Receive warnings of low stock based on current rate of sales and lead time for each vendor.

Sale item suggestions Pro

Using the sale suggestion page, know which products aren’t selling so you can release cash and inventory.

Inventory analysis Pro

Decide which products are worth reordering and which are taking up valuable storage and cash based with the stock performance grading system.

Stock adjustments Pro

Keep track of small changes made to your stock levels.

Inventory counts Pro

Use a barcode scanner to match your store inventory to your records.

Inventory receiving Pro

Perform accurate inventory counts for incoming orders with a barcode scanner.

Detailed inventory reports Pro

Identify trends and confidently plan for the future with reports about your locations and inventory.

Inventory tracking

Stay on top of your inventory and assign products to different sales channels and locations. Your count will automatically update when an order is fulfilled from that source.

Staff management

Control permissions and track staff performance from one place

Manager approvals Pro

Specify when your staff would need manager approval for actions like applying discounts or editing taxes.

Unlimited POS staff Pro

Let staff use POS while keeping admin access separate. Add as many employees as you like.

POS staff roles and permissions Pro

Assign custom roles to store staff ensuring they have the correct permissions.

Sales commissions Pro

Attribute sales to staff members for commissions or kudos.

Staff POS PINs

Give staff members unique PINs to log into POS.

Checkout

Complete each sale with a seamless experience for customers and staff

Returns and exchanges Pro

Accept returns and exchanges from any location, online or in store, and watch your inventory update automatically.

Cancel and void orders Pro

Cancel orders and void payments easily if a customer changes their mind or a mistake is made at checkout.

Draft orders Pro

Custom printed receipts Pro

Easily customize printed receipts to fit your brand. Plus, get access to the code editor for more advanced modifications.

Checkout validations Pro

Improve the data captured at checkout by enforcing cart actions like adding a customer to an order or attributing a sale to a staff member.

Customizable smart grid

Speed up checkout with the customizable smart grid that adapts based on cart actions to create cues for staff.

Add products to cart

Quickly build carts through search, customized product tiles, or scanning barcodes from your device camera or a connected barcode scanner.

Custom sale

Discounts

Apply dollar and percentage discounts at checkout by entering them manually or creating promotions that are automatically applied to qualifying orders.

Taxes

Custom email or SMS receipts

Send custom email or SMS receipts and build your customer contact list for re-engagement.

Customer receipt selection

Order notes

Track special requests and details by attaching notes to orders.

Tips

Pre-set dollar or percentage amounts to let customers show their appreciation at checkout.

Products

Stay organized with real-time product visibility across all your sales channels

Unlimited products

Add an unlimited number of products to your store and manage large product catalogs all in one place.

Product collections

Categorize products by type, season, promotions, and more. Use smart collections to automatically sort products based on vendor, price, and inventory level.

Product variants

Add multiple variations to products such as size, color, or materials and assign their own price, SKU, weight, and inventory.

Inventory states

Use inventory states to track and share the status of your inventory as products are received, transferred, or an order is fulfilled.

Multi-location management

Create and allocate inventory to your various online sales channels, warehouses, retail stores, or wherever else you store stock.

Barcode labels

Assign existing barcodes to products or create new ones to keep track of inventory and speed up checkout.

Email capture at checkout

Customer profiles

Order history

Customer metafields

Customer information

Marketing opt-ins

Frequently asked questions

Your point of sale should be the hub of your retail business, where you can quickly make sales and manage inventory, staff, orders, and more. Shopify keeps every element of your store at your fingertips so you can work faster and always have a clear view of your business.

The key features of Shopify POS include:

  • An intuitive and fast checkout
  • Fully integrated payments
  • Mobile POS hardware
  • Inventory management that syncs in-store and online
  • Complete customer profiles with order history
  • Custom staff permissions

A POS system is a crucial part of your inventory management strategy. Your point of sale should automatically update inventory levels for all of your products as items are sold, returned, exchanged, or transferred.

Shopify POS gives you even more control over inventory than a traditional POS system because inventory is synced across all locations where you have inventory—this could be multiple retail stores, warehouses, or even temporary pop-ups. No matter where you sell or store your products, you’ll always be in control of your inventory.

In addition to the features for selling in person with Shopify POS, every Shopify plan includes, ecommerce, payments processing, marketing, analytics, shipping tools, and more. When you choose Shopify, you get more than just a point of sale. You get everything you need to run your business and grow the way you want.
