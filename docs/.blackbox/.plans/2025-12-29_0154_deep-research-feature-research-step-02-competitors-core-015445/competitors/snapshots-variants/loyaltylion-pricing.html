Frequently asked questions

How does LoyaltyLions pricing work?

Our pricing is based on the average number of orders your store processes each month, allowing you to scale your loyalty program as your store grows. For some existing customers there are base order limits by plan, please see ‘Order thresholds for existing customers’ FAQ below for more details.

Order thresholds for existing customers

Over time our pricing plans have been updated and we now offer more flexibility within each plan. For existing customers, unless otherwise stated in your order form, the base order limits per plan are capped as follows:

Small Business: 800 orders
Classic: 2,000 orders
Advanced: 4,000 orders
Plus: 10,000+ orders

If youd like to review your current plan please get in touch to discuss your options.

What if my monthly order volume exceeds my plan limit?

If you exceed your contractually agreed monthly order threshold by more than 25% over an average of three consecutive months, then we give you a call to discuss whether youre on the most suitable Plan and/or Order threshold for your store.

This gives your Brand room to grow as well as headroom during the busy seasonal periods, safe in the knowledge that you’ll keep paying the same great price.

What ROI can I expect from LoyaltyLion?

Use our ROI calculator to estimate the incremental revenue you could generate by launching a loyalty program with LoyaltyLion. Based on real customer data, youll get a personalized projection tailored to your business.

What languages is LoyaltyLion available in?

Our software is available in 7 languages (Dutch, English, French, German, Italian, Spanish and Swedish).

You can also customise any text within your program, allowing you to translate it into any language you choose.

We currently serve ecommerce businesses in 51 countries.

What happens after onboarding?

No matter the Plan level that you select, you’ll continue to receive plenty of support once you’ve launched. All Plan levels have access to our Support team and our more advanced plans also have access to dedicated CSMs and Account Managers. The important thing to note is we will be monitoring your program and suggesting improvements, no matter what plan you’re on.

How easy is it to migrate my existing loyalty program to LoyaltyLion?

Migrating from another loyalty provider is quick and straightforward. Once your LoyaltyLion account is set up, well assist you in importing your existing customer data, ensuring a seamless transition without losing any valuable information.

Great! Take the next step bysigning up for a 30-minute callwith one of our loyalty experts who will take you through a live demo, provide industry-specific loyalty advice, answer your burning questions and create a personalized ROI quote.
