Frequently asked questions

Can I use my custom packaging?

Absolutely! We know that the branded and unboxing experience is a crucial opportunity to enhance your customer’s perception of your product and brand. Custom packages are simply billed as an additional item during the pick-and-pack process.

What are batches?

When fulfilling subscription boxes or crowdfunding orders, we set up an assembly line to kit “batches” of orders. A batch is a unique set of orders that contain the exact same combination of items. In order for the assembly line to be efficient, each batch must contain at least 50 orders. If not, there is a batching fee to compensate for setup of the smaller batch size.

Do you offer gift messaging? How much does it cost?

Yes! If your shopping cart supports gift messaging, we can include gift messages in orders and label them as “gift” orders. Here are the 2 options:
1) Print the gift message on a blank 4×6 card provided by ShipMonk.
2) Print the gift message on a custom 4×6 card provided by the merchant (additional pick fee applies).

Can you handle fragile products? Does it cost extra?

Yes, we offer fragile wrapping during the packing process. If you have specific requirements, let us know, and we can accommodate those. Our Happiness Engineers can also assist you with determining the best packaging option for your products. There are additional charges for fragile wrapping based on the product’s dimensions.

What is the monthly minimum & how is it calculated?

The Monthly Minimum is a standardized fee that is determined in conjunction with your pick fees and projected order volume. This fee ensures that we’re correctly:

 

  • Pricing your pick fees
  • Addressing seasonality
  • Planning labor
  • Making accurate volume guarantees to the shipping carriers

 

During the sales process, we’ll look at your typical monthly order volume and other factors to determine your pick fees. Once pick fees are established, we’ll calculate your Monthly Minimum. We do this by multiplying your monthly order volume by your first item pick fee and then reducing it by 20%

Do you offer storage insurance?

When you store your inventory at ShipMonk, you have two options to insure your product:


1) You add our location to your insurance policy
2) You choose to waive inventory insurance

 

Each option has its advantages and disadvantages. You can decide which option is best depending on your business’s setup and your product’s value.

What qualifies as a “special project”?

Special projects are one-off tasks that fall outside the scope of the regular pick and pack process or subscription box kitting and assembly. They include jobs like counting inventory, labeling, repackaging and/or bagging products, kitting and/or bundling, quality control, and other generic tasks. Special projects should be submitted to your Happiness Engineer, so they can define the scope and provide a quote for the project prior to commencing with the project.

Is there any cost for the ShipMonk software?

ShipMonk’s 3PL software is included at no charge for all clients, as well as all turnkey integrations. ShipMonk clients have access to the platform to manage inventory, orders, shipping, and more.

Does ShipMonk fulfill B2B/Wholesale/EDI orders?

Yes, ShipMonk can fulfill larger wholesale/retail orders. Click here for more information on our wholesale/B2B services.

How are shipping costs calculated?

Many factors determine the shipping cost of each package, including weight, dimensions, destination, and more. Contact us for a custom shipping analysis to see how much ShipMonk can save you on shipping.
