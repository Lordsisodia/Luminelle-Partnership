Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial?

You get 14 full days to explore Growave with no risk. Cancel anytime during the trial, no hidden fees, no hassle. If you uninstall before the trial ends, you won’t be charged.

Can you help me migrate from another app?

Yes, we make switching easy. You can export your customer data and import it into Growave using a simple CSV file. Or just reach out via live chat, our team is happy to assist with your migration instantly.

Do I need to sign a contract with Growave?

Not at all. Growave is commitment-free. You can start, pause, or cancel anytime. We believe in earning your trust with value, not long-term contracts.

How does the contract buyout work?

If you're locked into a contract with another loyalty or rewards app, we’ve got you covered. Growave will be completely free until your existing commitment ends. We'll match the length of your current contract so you can switch without paying twice.

How am I charged for Growave?

All charges are processed securely through Shopify’s billing system. Once your trial ends, your chosen plan is billed automatically. If you go beyond your order limit, Shopify applies an additional charge based on your usage.

I have multiple stores. Do you have any deals for subscribing to Growave on all my stores?

Yes! After you subscribe to a paid plan on your first store, contact us via chat or email to discuss pricing options.

What happens when I exceed my limits?

You will get charged for every additional 100 orders depending on your plan.
