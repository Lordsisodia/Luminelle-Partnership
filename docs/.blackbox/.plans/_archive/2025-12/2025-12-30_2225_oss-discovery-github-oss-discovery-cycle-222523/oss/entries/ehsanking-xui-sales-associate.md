## Repo description (from GitHub)

Automate creating/renewing Xray accounts on a **3x-ui** panel straight from **WooCommerce**. The plugin calls a secure **Cloudflare Worker**; the Worker logs into 3x-ui, adds/upserts the client, and returns subscription details to show in **My Account**.
