What is SAP Commerce Cloud?

SAP Commerce Cloud is a unified platform that delivers personalization and efficiency by fueling embedded AI with holistic, end-to-end business data.

From discovery to delivery, create effortless experiences at every step with tools that help you:

  • Grow across traditional e-commerce channels and emerging AI touchpoints.

  • Manage end-to-end B2B, B2C, and B2B2C commerce from one platform.

  • Uphold order delivery promises with agentic commerce-led scenario planning.

  • Unlock enterprise-wide AI use cases with SAP Business Suite connectivity.

Frequently asked questions

Commerce Cloud is our proven e-commerce solution builtmainly forlarge enterprises with advanced B2B, B2C, and B2B2C use cases. You can customize the solution to meet your specific needs and manage complex catalogs, products, and configurations for creating exceptional, hyper-personalized omnichannel experiences.

Commerce Cloud can help you ease the buying process for your customers with a smooth experience from search to sales. A combination of intuitive self-service capabilities and AI-enhancedmerchandising, guided selling,assistedservice, and chatbots further aid the buying process and drive more profit at a lower cost of ownership. Commerce Cloud also simplifies complex e-commerce and order processes, so you can engage customers more effectively and connect demand with your supply chain.

Prebuilt integration is based on SAP Integration Suite, which helps connect applications with other SAP and third-party cloud and on-premises applications. Since Commerce Cloud is based on open APIs, you can quickly integrate it with third-party solutions as well. We offer many prebuilt integrations to third-party solutions throughSAPStore. You can also use serverless microservices to extend your e-commerce architecture through the open-source project Kyma.

Yes, Commerce Cloud can enable headless e-commerce scenarios through an API-first approach and the decoupled JavaScript-based storefront layer. This enables the creation of exceptional e-commerce experiences on every channel, device, or location, without requiring updates to the back-end core e-commerce software.

Yes, Commerce Cloud provides an embedded solution for product, content, and catalog management.

The Product Content Management solution enables you to consolidate product data and catalogs centrally and publish them in various channels. You can make edits through an intuitive user interface, enhance product content quality with built-in data validation and workflows, bulk-edit and mass-upload product data, and support multilingual catalogs for a global e-commerce site.
