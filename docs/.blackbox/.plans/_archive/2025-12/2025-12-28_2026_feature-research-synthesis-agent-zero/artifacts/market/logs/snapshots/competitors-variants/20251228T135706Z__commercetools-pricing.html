TAILOR YOUR PLAN
Flexible technology, flexible packages: Add-ons made easy.

Customize your commercetools package by mixing and matching capabilities, and create the perfect solution for your success.

Premium Support and Audit Log Premium

With Premium Support, you can enjoy enhanced SLA, priority support, and personalized guidance. Audit Log Premium ensures transparency by tracking changes across your commerce operations.

Advanced B2B APIs

Unlock robust B2B capabilities: Model any account hierarchy with Business Units and determine granular-role user permissions for purchasing processes. Buyer approval flows, quote management and more are also available.

Additional regions, connectors and more

Expand to new regions, integrate best-of-breed connectors and cost-effectively scale everything else you need. Scale your business with all the tools to support your growth.

Checkout

From carts and order management to payments, manage the entire checkout journey seamlessly from a single solution. Integrate effortlessly with leading PSPs, tailor shipping and tax settings, and offer features like shopping lists and wishlists.

Performance testing

Get ready for multiple peak sales events and proactively address potential issues for seamless rollouts. With Performance testing, you can ensure your system is prepared for any demand. This add-on is available with the Premium package.

HIPAA compliance

Healthcare businesses can leverage our HIPAA compliance to secure the processing of PHI (protected health information) in the US market and safeguard sensitive data in every transaction. This add-on is available with the Premium package.
