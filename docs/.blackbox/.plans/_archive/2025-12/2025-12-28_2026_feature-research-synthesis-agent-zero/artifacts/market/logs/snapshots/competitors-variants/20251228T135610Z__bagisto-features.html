Frequently Asked Questions

Need help choosing the right Laravel eCommerce Features or want to know more? Here are some common questions we get asked.

What makes Bagisto different from other eCommerce platforms?

Bagisto is Open Source, fully customizable, and packed with features like multivendor support, product SEO, and PWA. It gives you the flexibility to build the store you want.

Can I build a marketplace with Bagisto?

Yes, with features like the Multivendor Marketplace, you can turn your store into a complete marketplace with multiple sellers managing their own products.

Do I need coding skills to use Bagisto?

No, you can set up and manage most of your store without coding. But if you want custom features, you can work with developers or use extensions.

Does Bagisto support multiple languages and currencies?

Yes, it has built-in multi-locale and multi-currency support, so you can easily sell to customers around the world.

Can I improve my store’s SEO with Bagisto?

Yes, it includes SEO tools like meta titles, descriptions, keywords, and SEO-friendly URLs to help your store show up in search engines.

How can Generative AI help in my store?

Generative AI can help you quickly write product descriptions, content ideas, and more—saving you time and effort.

What payment options can I connect with Bagisto?

You can integrate trusted payment partners like PayPal, Paymob, and Pay. It also supports many other local and global payment gateways.

Can I sell on other platforms with Bagisto?

Yes, with multichannel selling, you can connect your store to marketplaces, apps, and social media to reach more customers.
