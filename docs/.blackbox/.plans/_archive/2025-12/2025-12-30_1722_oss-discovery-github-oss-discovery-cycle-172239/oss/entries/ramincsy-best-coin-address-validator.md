## Repo description (from GitHub)

Best-Coin-Address-Validator is an open-source console app in C# for validating digital currency addresses. Its secure algorithms ensure valid addresses for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and more. With a simple UI, it's easy to integrate into other apps, giving users confidence in trustworthy addresses. It's cross-platform and viewable on GitHub.
