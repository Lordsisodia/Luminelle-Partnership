Discovery Box FAQs

Is this instant or freeze-dried?

No. We capture coffee at its absolute peakso you can taste it at its best. Each roast is precision-brewed to 10x strength, then flash-frozen at -321F to preserve every flavor compound, aromatic note, and nuance. This process locks in freshness without oxidation or bitterness, delivering a smooth, vibrant cup that tastes like it was just brewedno matter when you drink it.

Why are your capsules frozen?

Each coffee is brewed to 10x strength, then flash-frozen at -321F to preserve its full flavor and aroma. The result? The freshest, most vibrant cup youve ever tastedready in seconds.

Weve done the hard work so you get a perfectly delicious cup, every time. No machine, gear, or learning curvejust melt, sip, and enjoy exceptional coffee in seconds.

What happens if capsules melt?

Capsules can be refrozen if not used immediately. Liquid capsules maintain peak flavor for up to 24 hours at room temperature or 3 days in the refrigerator. That's why Cometeer is great for on the go. Pro tip: we're TSA approved!

How does the Tasting Guide work?

James Hoffmannrenowned coffee expert and World Barista Championpersonally selected each of these eight coffees in collaboration with top roasters to help you experience the full spectrum of specialty coffee. With each cup, youll get a short video from James, guiding you through the tasting experience and helping you understand exactly what makes each roast special.

With each coffee, youll be prompted to rate what youre tasting and track your preferences along the way. At the end, well send you a personalized flavor profile based on your ratingsso you can keep discovering coffees that match your unique taste.

How sustainable is it?

We created a curbside-recyclable aluminum capsule, and didnt stop there all of our packaging and shipping materials are recyclable. And, because we deal with the grounds on our end by composting them the capsules are easy to just drop in the recycling when youre done with them. We also completely eliminate waste from beans or ground coffee going stale on-shelf and optimize the yield of each bean (so nothing is wasted from poor brewing).

How do you make it?

Weve already brewed each cup to perfection and frozen it to preserve all the depth and flavorso you can enjoy barista-level taste at home with zero effort.

Just melt the capsule and experience an exceptional cup, every time. No machines, no mess, no compromise.

How to melt

1

Grab a thawed capsule; to thaw leave in the fridge overnight or submerge in hot water for 5 minutes.

2

Peel off the lid.

3

Pour into 6-8 oz. of your favorite liquid (milk, water, etc.), stir, and enjoy!

How to melt

1

Grab the capsule of your choice from the freezer.

2

Peel off the lid, and push the button on the bottom to pop out the puck.

3

Pour 6-8 oz. of hot water, stir, and enjoy!

MORE RECIPES

Iced Coffee

Pour melted coffee into 6 - 8 oz of water and ice.

Hot Coffee

Combine frozen coffee puck with 6 - 8 oz of hot water.

Iced Latte

Pour melted coffee into 6 oz of milk or dairy alternative, add ice.

Hot Latte

Pour melted coffee into mug and add 6 oz of steamed milk or dairy alternative.

Affogato

Pour melted coffee over a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
