Quality you can taste

Were so confident in the quality of our produce and groceries, were putting our money on the line. If anything in your delivery doesnt live up to our quality standards, well make it right ASAP.

What makes it "imperfect"?

  • Leftover ends & pieces

    Producers are often left with manufacturing byproducts that arent typically sold.

  • Discoloration

    We know that a little variation in color is totally normal (and has no impact on taste!).

  • Excess inventory

    Sometimes farmers and producers need a little extra help selling through their items.

  • Packaging issues

    Supermarkets often wont accept outdated branding on packaging, but thats where we come in.

  • Near best-by date

    Grocery stores dont tend to like items with approaching best-bys, which means we get to offer em at a discount.

  • Too big, too small

    When weather is unpredictable, our farmers often end up with produce thats a little irregular in size.

Hungry for more?

We are a grocery delivery service on a mission to reduce food waste. Shopping with us is simple.

  1. Sign up and share how you like to shop through a quick quiz.
  2. We assign you a shopping window and delivery day, based on where you live.
  3. Each week, well start your cart pre-filled with food we think youll like based on your preferences and whats in season. Youre free to add and remove items to build the perfect order for you.
  4. We deliver your order on the same day every week to reduce emissions.
