Farm-to-Closet Values

Reciprocal

Through creating intimate, mutually beneficial relationships with the communities and ecosystems that support us, we can support them.

Regenerative

Regenerative farming actively heals- it increases biodiversity, creates resilient ecosystems, and sequesters atmospheric carbon into the soil.

Ethical

Everyone from Farm-to-Closet makes a living wage, farmers gain financial independence, and artisans have steady work.

Evolutionary

Farm-to-Closet is a roadmap for a new way that we hope many follow. Together, we can heal Mother Earth.
