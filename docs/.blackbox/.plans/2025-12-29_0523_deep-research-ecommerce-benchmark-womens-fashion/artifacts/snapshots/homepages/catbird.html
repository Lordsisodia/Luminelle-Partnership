World of Catbird

Love letters from a tiny shop, born in Williamsburg in 2004.

Welcome to Catbird, your destination for the finest 14k solid gold jewelry and gifts for all, lovingly designed in Brooklyn since 2004 and made from over 95% recycled gold & recycled diamonds. Explore our range of 14k gold rings, including signet and stacking rings, all designed to layer and wear over time. You can shop all of our jewelry here, as well as our collection of silver jewelry.

Build your own necklace stack with our assortment of solid gold chains, lockets, name jewelry, and diamond necklaces - or layer your ear stack with our edit of hoop earrings, diamond earrings, drops, pearls and studs. Each piece pairs effortlessly, allowing you to create a personalized look unique to you.

Discover the art of jewelry stacking with our recycled 14k gold bracelets,tennis bracelets, and personalized engraved gold bracelets. Since our jewelry is made with solid gold, you never have to take it off.

Celebrate love and mark lifes milestones with our wedding ring collection, featuring recycled diamond engagement rings, and lab-grown diamond rings, all sustainably sourced and designed to last a lifetime.
