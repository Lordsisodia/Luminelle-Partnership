## What to capture per store (minimum set)

- Homepage (optional): hero + nav entry points
- PLP / collection: filters visible + quick-add/variant handling
- PDP: media, size module, reviews, shipping/returns summary
- Cart: variant editing, shipping threshold messaging, upsells
- Checkout start: express pay, delivery estimates, trust cues
- Returns policy / returns portal: clarity + self-serve flow entry point
