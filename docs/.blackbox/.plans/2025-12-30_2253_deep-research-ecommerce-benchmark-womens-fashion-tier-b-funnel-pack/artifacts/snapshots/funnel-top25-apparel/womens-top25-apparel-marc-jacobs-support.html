  • My payment declined but I still see a charge. What should I do?

    • Check that the payment method is active and not expired.
    • Ensure your security number is correct.

    • Reach out to your bank after two declines for assistance, continuing to try may result in further declines and authorization holds.

  • How is tax calculated?

    State laws require that we charge applicable sales tax on orders shipped to the states where Marc Jacobs has a presence.

    Orders shipped to Canada will be subject to duties, customs tariffs, value added taxes and similar taxes that are levied are required in the applicable jurisdiction by local authorities, which will be in addition to the prices for the merchandise listed on our website and will be collected during checkout.

    When placing an order on marcjacobs.com, you (the customer) are considered the importer of record and must comply with all applicable laws and regulations. All charges for duties, taxes and the carrier's collection fee are in addition to your shipping charge and shall be paid exclusively by you and will be reflected on your invoice.

    You should contact your local customs office for further information. The amount of any applicable duties, tariffs and taxes will vary based on a multitude of factors, including items such as the destination of origin or the classification of the merchandise being purchased.

  • Canvas goods Care Instructions

    Our canvas goods are crafted using special materials, please handle them with extra care.

    When not in use, we recommend that you stuff your bag to preserve the shape and store it in a cool dark place to ensure it remains beautiful for as long as possible. Please avoid contact with other dyed materials and avoid over exposure to heat and direct light as color fading will occur. Please avoid getting the canvas wet as it can stain the garment. We do not recommend the use of any cleaning agents on our fabrics.

    Printed canvas goods are a delicate material and can suffer from abrasion, cracking and wear if not handled properly. Salt and atmospheric agents in general can cause irreversible wear. Please avoid contact with heat, oil, grease, alcohol and other abrasive products.

    Natural canvas is meant to break in and wear with use, any time-lapsed variance should not be viewed as a defect, but rather as the beauty of a customized product.

  • Leather goods Care Instructions

    Our leather goods are crafted using special materials, please handle them with extra care.

    When not in use, we recommend you stuff your bag and shoes to preserve the shape and store it in a cool dark place to ensure it remains beautiful for as long as possible. Please avoid contact with dyed materials and harsh chemicals. If leather should get wet, let it dry and rub gently with soft cloth to maintain the original color and avoid water spots.

    Naturally tanned leathers will gradually darken in color over time and with exposure to light. They also may have incidental tonal variations, marks or veins which are natural features and should not be considered imperfections.

    Printed leather goods are a delicate material and can suffer from abrasion, cracking and wear if not handled properly. Salt and atmospheric agents in general can cause irreversible wear. Please avoid contact with heat, oil, grease, alcohol and other abrasive products.

    Delicate leather goods that are constructed using any delicate leathers should be handled carefully. Please avoid contact with light colored clothing and items, rain, heat, oil, grease and other abrasive products. Preserve your item inside a dustbag when not in use.

  • Denim goods Care Instructions

    Due to the nature and finish of our denim, color may transfer into light colored fabric, please use care when wearing. Avoid repeated contact with other dyed material and avoid over exposure to heat and direct light as fading may occur.

  • Embellished goods Care Instructions

    Our embellished goods are constructed using delicate materials and we recommend you handle them very carefully. Some embellishments, such as sequins, glitter, crystals, stones, studs, beads, embroideries, patches, charms and pom poms are delicate additions that must be handled with care to preserve the items. Please avoid contact with any objects and avoid overstuffing leather and fabric accessories.

    When not in use, please store it in a protective dust bag and avoid close contact with other objects. We recommend you use these items gently and avoid any over exposure to light or moisture.

  • Jewelry goods Care Instructions

    Our jewelry is made of delicate materials. We recommend proper use and storage to keep items from tarnishing. Please avoid direct contact with water, perfume, body lotion, make-up, hand sanitizer and other chemicals. Please remove jewelry before participating in physical activities such as swimming, exercise, cleaning or anything that causes excessive perspiration. We recommend you store it in a dustbag or a cool, dry place. When not in use and avoiding direct sunlight, humidity and sleeping in your items.

  • Ready to Wear Care Instructions

    Our ready-to-wear pieces are created with high quality materials, we encourage you to take great care of them to ensure longevity. We recommend you store your items in a dry and cool space away from humidity and exposure to light. For higher end pieces, we recommend storing them in a garment bag made of cloth.

    For our denim, please keep away from light and other garment dyed items to preserve its dye process.

    We advise that you please follow all instructions inside each specific item on the tag provided. Each item will have careful instructions for care and cleaning. After reviewing the care label if the instructions advise to machine wash, then it's important to always use cold water. Always wash with similar colors and then lay flat to dry. Do not wring or twist.
