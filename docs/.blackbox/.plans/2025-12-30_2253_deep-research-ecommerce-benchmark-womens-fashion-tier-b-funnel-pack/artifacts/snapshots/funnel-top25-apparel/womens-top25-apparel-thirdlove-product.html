FAQs

When is it best to wear a T-shirt bra?

A T-shirt bra is a great choice for almost any outfit or occasion, offering great shape and comfortable support.

Does the T-shirt bra also push up breasts?

The T-shirt bra does not have bump pads to add a push-up effect. However, the 24/7® Classic T-Shirt Bra still delivers great lift and shape without extra padding.

What are the benefits of ThirdLove's T-shirt bra?

The only T-shirt bra made in half cup sizes, ThirdLove’s 24/7® T-Shirt Bra offers a superior fit. It also has memory foam cups that mold to your shape and no-slip straps.

How much padding is in the 24/7® T-Shirt Bra?

The ultra-thin memory foam cups in the 24/7® T-Shirt Bra deliver great shape and a touch of modesty without adding bulk.

What breast shape is best for T-shirt bras?

ThirdLove bras will work for most, if not all, breast shapes. Athletic, bell, east-west, relaxed, and round breast shapes may work especially well with a T-shirt bra.

How do I find the right T-shirt bra size?

Our Fitting Room Quiz can tell you in less than 2 minutes, but if you already know your size in a different ThirdLove bra, that is also your T-shirt bra size.
