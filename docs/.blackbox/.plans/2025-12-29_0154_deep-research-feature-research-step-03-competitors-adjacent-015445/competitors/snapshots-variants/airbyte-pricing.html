Capacity Based Pricing

Most data movement solutions today are priced based on the amount of data you are moving. But we know that not all data is equally valuable, and volumes of data moved can be very unpredictable.

A better pricing model

With our Plus and Pro plans, Airbyte uses a capacity model built on Data Workers—dedicated units of compute that power your pipelines.

Your cost depends on how many pipelines you run in parallel, making pricing predictable while giving you the flexibility to scale capacity as your workloads grow.

This way, you always know your costs up front, without unpredictable volume-based overages.

A note on volume-based pricing

For some individuals and smaller teams, volume-based pricing still works for their needs. This is why our Standard plan still uses volume as its key metric for determining price.

Choose the plan that fits your team’s needs: predictable, scalable capacity for Plus and Pro, or typical volume-based pricing with Standard.

What plans does Airbyte offer, and how do they differ?

Click HERE to scroll up and see all of the plans Airbyte currently offers along with a useful comparison matrix.

What is capacity-based pricing (Data Workers)?

Airbyte’s Pro and Plus plans are priced by Data Workers, a unit of capacity that powers your pipelines. There are multiple variables that we use to determine the cost of a Data Worker.

Each Data Worker can run multiple syncs concurrently — typically ~3 at once.

This model gives you:

  • Predictable spend
  • Guaranteed performance
  • No charges based on data volume

How is capacity-based pricing different from usage or volume-based?

Usage or volume-based pricing bills you by rows, GBs, or events. Costs grow as data grows and we know that not all data is equally valuable.

Airbyte uses capacity, so you:

  • Control spend up-front
  • Avoid “data tax” and surprise bills
  • Can scale only when needed

Your data can grow without inflating your bill.

What is the Plus plan?

Plus is our annual plan for teams looking for Standard functionality but would like to be billed on a predictable, annual basis. Plus also comes with Accelerated Support that means your tickets are prioritized in the queue.

It includes:

  • Dedicated Standard Data Workers
  • Accelerated support
  • Annual billing

How much does Airbyte Plus cost?

Pricing is based on the number of Data Workers needed.

Start with a single worker — scale up when needed.

What is the Pro plan?

Pro includes everything in Standard/Plus, plus:

  • Faster syncs
  • Premium support 
  • Enhanced platform features like RBAC, row filtering, hashing & encryption, and multiple data regions

It’s built for production workloads that require reliability, control, and guaranteed performance.

For a small team (3–4 developers), what do you recommend?

We typically recommend starting with:

  • Plus (fully managed) if you want no infrastructure management and predictable annual spend

Either way, you can increase capacity or move to more robust pricing plans like Pro as you scale and your needs change.

Can Airbyte provide a tailored quote?

Yes. Tell us about your pipelines and we’ll size your Data Worker needs and provide a tailored quote.
