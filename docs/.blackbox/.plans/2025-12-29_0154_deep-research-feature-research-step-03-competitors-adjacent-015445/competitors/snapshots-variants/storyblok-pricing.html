Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about our product, pricing and terminology.

  • 01 Are the subscriptions per project?

    Yes, our subscriptions are based on spaces. One account can create multiple spaces and upgrade for those you need more.

  • 02 Is there a setup fee?

    There are no setup fees for our self-service customers at all. You can signup and get started today with your headless CMS.

  • 03 Do I need credit card details to sign up?

    No! We have a free plan that you can use to try out Storyblok and even to build great websites. You can sign up and start working instantly without entering your credit card details. You will however have to add them once your 100GB of free traffic is close to be consumed or if you want to upgrade your space subscription.

  • 04 What are apps?

    In Storyblok, apps bring functionality such as responsive previews, advanced collaborators and approval workflows, as well as external services like our integrated search functionality. You can remove and add functionality to fit your own requirements. The full list of apps can be found in app.storyblok.com.

  • 05 How do I change my subscription?

    As an owner, you can change your plan. Go to Settings - Subscription - Choose your plan. Choose the desired plan, select the seats, the paying interval and add the billing information.

  • 06 What are your uptimes?

    Check our status at: uptime.storyblok.com. In our terms, you can find our API uptime percentage per year.

  • 07 Where can I find the terms?

    You will come across our terms of service during the signup process, or by using the provided link in the footer or in this FAQ.

  • 08 What about traffic?

    Our subscriptions come with an included traffic allowance per month. Everything after that will be billed by the following:

    • Starter, Growth & Growth Plus: 75/month for 250GB of additional traffic
  • 09 What about VAT?

    You can add and update your VAT number in your payment settings and will see your tax percentage before subscribing.

  • 10 Where can I find the Data Processing Agreement?

    You can download our DPA via your in-app profile. Have a look at our privacy policy, terms and other legal documents to know more about us. If you have any questions regarding those topics do not hesitate and chat with us using our live-chat.

  • 11 What about videos?

    Assets upload: Max size of 500MB per asset on Starter, 1GB for Growth & Growth Plus, and 5GB on Enterprise.

  • 12 What about static sites?

    Storyblok only delivers JSON and therefore can be combined with any static site generator you want. We‚Äôve created a tutorial for Jekyll and others will follow in our Stories section for NuxtJS, Metalsmith, and more.

  • 13 Are there asset type upload limitations?

    For security purposes, Storyblok reserves the right to restrict certain file types in unverified spaces. If the asset type you intend to upload is not supported, please reach out to our support team to start the verification process for your space.
