Frequently asked questions

What happens if I run out of AI credits?

While you can continue to use all other ToolJet features, you can contact us at help@tooljet.com to purchase extra AI credits as per your need.

How to purchase additional credits?

Please reach out to us at hello@tooljet.com.

Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?

Yes, you can change plans at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Do unused AI credits roll over?

No, AI credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. This helps us ensure consistent service quality for all users.

Is it possible to upgrade from self-hosted community edition?

Yes! It won't take more than a few minutes to upgrade from the community edition to enterprise edition.

Can I deploy ToolJet on-premise?

Yes! both the community edition and enterprise edition of Tooljet can be deployed on-premise. You can also opt for air-gapped deployment to run ToolJet without internet access. It usually takes less than 30 minutes to deploy ToolJet for the first time.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14 day free trial period for both business and enterprise edition. Reach out to our team for a license key.

Do you have special pricing for open source / non-profit projects?

Yes, we offer heavy discounts for open source projects. Contact us at hello@tooljet.com with the details of the project to receive this discount.
