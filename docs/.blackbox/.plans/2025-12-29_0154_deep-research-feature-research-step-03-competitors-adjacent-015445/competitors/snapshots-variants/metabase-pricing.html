Frequently asked questions

What’s the difference between Pro and Enterprise?

Feature wise: zero. With Enterprise you get assigned a dedicated success engineer and a 1-day email SLA response time making it a great option for customers that run Metabase in mission-critical or sensitive environments.

How are users calculated?

Everyone who signs into your Metabase (logging in directly or via SSO) is a user for purposes of billing. Licenses are for individuals and are non-transferrable. Learn more about how we define users and how billing works.

Can I adjust the number of users I’m paying for?

Yes. In your instance you can add or deactivate any users. Your billing will automatically be prorated. Learn more about billing for new or deactivated users.

How does pricing work for embedding?

Depends on what kind of embedding you’re doing. Interactive Embedding requires viewers to sign in, which means they will count as users for billing purposes. Static Embedding doesn’t require viewers to sign in, which means they won’t count as additional users for billing purposes.

Since per-seat pricing is optimized for internal analytics and may not fit your revenue model when embedding, we work with our Enterprise customers to find Interactive Embedding pricing structures that work for their business models. Get in touch to learn more about Enterprise.

Does it cost more money to create more visualizations, run more queries, connect to additional data sources, etc.?

No. You can make as many dashboards, visualizations, collections, queries as you like and connect to as many data sources as you have. Metabase costs the same no matter what you make, or how much you explore.
