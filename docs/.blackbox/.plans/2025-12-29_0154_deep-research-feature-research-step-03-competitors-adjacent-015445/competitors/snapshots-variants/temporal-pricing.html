YOUR WORKFLOW BUILDING BLOCKS

Actions

Actions represent billable operations between your application and Temporal Cloud.

Write your business logic as code in a Temporal Workflow using our open-source SDKs, and the Temporal Service maps your Workflow Events to Temporal Cloud Actions to track your usage.

CHOOSE HOW YOU SCALE

Capacity

By default, all Namespaces start with On-Demand mode at 500 APS, scaling automatically based on 7-day usage.

Optionally, you can switch to Provisioned* to set capacity with Temporal Resource Units (TRUs), each adding 500 APS. You'll get a base number of Actions/hr, and pay only for usage beyond that.
